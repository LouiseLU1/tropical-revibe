Who We Are

Conscious living,
beautifully redefined.

Tropical ReVibe is an eco-friendly and zero-waste store located in Liwliwa, Zambales — a place where nature and community come together.

Inspired by the beauty of the beach, we offer sustainable alternatives for everyday living, from personal care to lifestyle essentials. Rooted in mindful living, our goal is to make sustainability both accessible and appealing. Whether you're a traveler, a surfer, or a local, Tropical ReVibe invites you to experience a more conscious way of living by the sea.

Our Story

History

The Beginning

Born from a deep love for nature and a passion for mindful living, Tropicals ReVibe was founded by a Zambales native whose adventurous spirit and strong connection to the beach shaped the brand's identity.

The Inspiration

Growing up surrounded by the ocean and exploring coastal landscapes, she witnessed both the beauty of nature and the growing impact of waste on these environments. This sparked a vision for more conscious everyday choices.

The Vision

This led to the creation of Tropicals ReVibe — an eco-friendly and zero-waste store dedicated to sustainable alternatives, from personal care to lifestyle essentials, each product carefully selected for a more mindful way of living.

More than just a store, Tropicals ReVibe serves as a platform to inspire others to embrace a lifestyle that respects and protects the environment. Rooted in its founder's love for adventure and the sea, the brand continues to advocate for sustainability, one small change at a time.

What Drives Us
